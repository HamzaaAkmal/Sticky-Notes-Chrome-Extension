# Privacy Policy for Sticky Notes Extension

**Last Updated:** November 18, 2025

## Overview
Sticky Notes - Easy Web Notes & Quick Reminders ("the Extension") respects your privacy. This policy explains what data we collect and how we use it.

## Data Collection

### What We Collect
- **Sticky Notes Content:** The text content of notes you create
- **Note Metadata:** Website URLs where notes are saved, note positions, and timestamps
- **Authentication Data:** If you choose to sign in with Google, we store your Google user ID and email for cloud sync

### What We DON'T Collect
- We do NOT collect your browsing history beyond the URLs where you create notes
- We do NOT track your activity on websites
- We do NOT collect personal information, financial data, or health information
- We do NOT use analytics or tracking cookies

## How We Use Your Data

### Local Storage
By default, all notes are stored locally in your browser using Chrome's storage API. This data never leaves your device unless you explicitly enable cloud sync.

### Cloud Sync (Optional)
If you choose to sign in with Google:
- Your notes are backed up to our secure cloud storage
- Data is encrypted in transit using HTTPS
- You can disable cloud sync anytime from the extension settings

## Data Sharing
- We do NOT sell your data to third parties
- We do NOT share your data with advertisers
- We do NOT use your data for purposes unrelated to the extension's functionality
- Your notes data is only used to provide sticky notes functionality

## Data Security
- All data transmission uses HTTPS encryption
- Cloud-synced notes are stored securely
- You control your data and can delete it anytime

## Your Rights
You have the right to:
- Access your data through the extension dashboard
- Delete your notes anytime (locally or from cloud)
- Disable cloud sync and use only local storage
- Uninstall the extension, which removes all local data

## Third-Party Services
The extension uses:
- **Google OAuth:** For optional login (Google's Privacy Policy applies)
- **Chrome Storage API:** For local and sync storage (Google's Privacy Policy applies)

## Children's Privacy
This extension does not knowingly collect data from children under 13.
